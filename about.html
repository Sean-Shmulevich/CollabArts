Who We Are

Collab Arts is a student-run multidisciplinary art collective that produces meaningful youthful experiential experiences by utilizing multimedia arts, community culture, media design, and technology. The collective bridges the gap between emerging artists, designers, and producers of different disciplines and levels of experience by hosting creative team-building workshops and themed interactive exhibitions.
