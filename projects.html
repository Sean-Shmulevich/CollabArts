ECO-JAM

Promo

Description

Collab Arts At Pitt presents Eco-Village,

A sustainability-oriented district at the Rhythm and Threads @rhythm.and.threads festival.

Eco-Jam is a creative reuse festival featuring live street art, thrift & art pop-ups, performances, skate and music. Come celebrate collaborative arts and making art from junk with us! RSVP now via link in our bio.
