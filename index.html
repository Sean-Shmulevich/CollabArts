Vision

    At Collab Arts, we envision ourselves at the forefront of a new wave of creativity, where big ideas intersect with social consciousness. Our goal is to inspire change and drive innovation, setting a new standard for collaborative creativity in our communities and beyond.

Mission

    We are a campus-oriented creative collective offering students with opportunities to collaborate and craft experiential art and experiences. Our work blends the art of ‘play’ with community engagement and social impact.

Values

    Collab Arts is more than an art collective; it's a community. We foster a culture of collaboration and empowerment, where every individual is encouraged to share their unique perspective.

Interactive Demo

To showcase an artistic collaboration, we have created an interactive demo that allows you to control the animation on the collabartspgh.com homepage using your camera.

We are using a machine learning model to track your hand movements. This model is running locally on your browser and does not store any data.

Instructions

  1. Click the "Enable Camera" button to start the camera.
    Your browser will also prompt you to enable camera access
  2. Hold your hand about 3-5 feet away from your webcam and slowly move your hand
  3. You will notice that the animation can be controlled with the movement of your hand
  4. Try Opening and closing your hand for an extra effect
  5. After enabling camera you can close this window
